Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I pay my rent online?

    Go to your dashboard, click on "Upcoming Rent," and then click "Pay Now" next to the relevant property.

  • What if I have a maintenance request?

    Please contact your landlord directly using their provided contact information.

  • How do I update my profile information?

    Go to your profile page and click on the "Edit Profile" button.
